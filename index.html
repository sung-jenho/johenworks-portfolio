About Me

My Journey

My journey into web development began with a curiosity about how websites work. What started as tinkering with HTML and CSS evolved into a passion for creating seamless digital experiences. Over the past year, I've immersed myself in modern web technologies, building projects that challenge me to grow and learn continuously.

What Drives Me

I'm driven by the intersection of design and functionality. I believe great web applications should not only look beautiful but also be intuitive, accessible, and performant. Every project is an opportunity to craft something that makes a real difference in users' lives.

My Approach

I approach problem-solving methodically: understand the challenge, research solutions, prototype quickly, and iterate based on feedback. I'm a strong believer in clean code, responsive design, and continuous learning. Whether it's debugging a complex issue or implementing a new feature, I tackle each challenge with curiosity and determination.
